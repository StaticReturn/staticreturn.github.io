# Health, Healing, Death

## Health
Your health is measured in hit points, the amount of times you can be wounded.  Once your number of wounds equals your hit points you fall to the ground dead.

## Death
A dead character will manifest a new body three hours after he dies at the anchor of his choice that is not in combat.  If the player has no living anchors he will manifest up to 10 miles from his death after the period of 1 month.

## Anchors
You must tell the story of your death to another immortal to create an anchor to them.

## Healing
A character heals:
* One condition per round.
* One wound per hour.

## True Death
The only way a character may experience true death is to have his head severed by the action and intent of another immortal within melee range.
