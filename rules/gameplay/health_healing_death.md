# Health, Healing, Death

## Health
The health of a character is measured in conditions.

## Healing
A player character heals:
* One minor condition per minute.
* One major condition per 10 minutes.
* One critical condition per hour.

## Death
A dead player character will manifest a new body three hours after he dies.
