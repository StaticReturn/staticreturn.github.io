### Friday Night Dungeon looks to accomplish the following:
* Fun, speedy combat.
* Roleplay focus.
* Simple.

### Problem Solving
Most modern TTRPGs focus on problem solving with your character sheet.  There is a trap?  Check which charcter has the biggest number and then have him roll dice.  Do math.  Was the player clever?  If so, add a small modification to the math.  Compare the result to the rules and you've got your outcome.

This is okay, but not what excites me.  I would rather play a game where I play a person in various circumstances and I have to solve problems with my decision making.  My character sheet lets me know what my character is good at, but what really drives the story are my choices and creativity.
